Lasagna Recipe

Extra Tasty Lasagna

You know the feeling: Your energy is running low, your family's hunger is running high, and dinner should have been on the table an hour ago. That's when you reach for this easy lasagna recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 medium carrot
  • 1 celery stick
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1kg lean beef mince
  • 800g canned crushed tomatos
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/2 tsp salt and pepper
  • 60g butter
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 4 cups milk
  • 2 cups of cheese
  • 350g fresh lasagna sheets

Fish Curry

Mouth-Watering Fish Curry

https://www.indianhealthyrecipes.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/fish-curry-recipe.jpg

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp peanut oil
  • 1 brown oinion, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, thinly sliced
  • 1 zucchini, thinly sliced
  • 400g butternut pumpkin, cut into 2cm pieces
  • 2 tbsp yellow curry paste
  • 400ml can coconut milk
  • 700g white fish fillets, cut into 3cm pieces
  • 500g steamed basmati rice
  • 1/2 cup coriander leaves
  • 1 tbsp fried shallots
